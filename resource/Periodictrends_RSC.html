Periodic Table Trends

Source: Royal Society of Chemistry

Description: This handy interactive periodic table allows you to intuitively visualize periodic trends in density, atomic radius, electronegativity, melting point, boiling point, and first ionization energy on the actual periodic table itself. You can also click each element to take you to a new page that describes that element's properties, uses, and history.

Quote: "The non-bonded atomic radius of an atom is half of the distance between two unbonded atoms of the same element when the electrostatic forces are balanced."

Ionic Sudoku

Source: Royal Society of Chemistry

Description: This worksheet has 3 puzzles that test students' knowledge of common cations and anions, and how to write chemical formulaes for ionic compounds based on those ions' charges. Includes answer sheets.

Quote: "Work out the compounds in the blank squares. Every row, column and 3x3 box contains a chloride, bromide, iodide, oxide, hydroxide, nitrate, carbonate, sulfate and phosphate."

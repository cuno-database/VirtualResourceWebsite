Block Color Game

Source: Legends of Learning

Description: This simple game from Legends of Learning lets players use Newton's Third Law to push different groups of blocks into the correct slots. The levels get increasingly challenging, and students will enjoy adjusting their strategies and using Newton's Third Law in different ways to achieve their objective.

Quote: It determines which way and how much force the Red Block has when it's going to launch. THe longer the line, the more force it will get.

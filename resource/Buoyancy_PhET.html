Buoyancy

Source: PhET

Description: This simple simulation from PhET allows students to play with different materials (and even create their own!) to determine what forces determine a materials buoyancy in different fluid substances.

Quote: When will objects float and when will they sink? Learn how buoyancy works with blocks. Arrows show the applied forces, and you can modify the properties of the blocks and the fluid.

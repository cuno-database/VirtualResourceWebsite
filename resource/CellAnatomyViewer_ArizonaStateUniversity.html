Cell Anatomy Viewer

Source: Arizona State University

Description: This resource allows students to learn about structures in cells of animals, plants, fungi, and bacteria at several levels. At the 'Explore' level, students can click on different organelles and read descriptions about their functions. At the 'Cell Parts ID Game' level, students will be play a game that involves identifying organelles based on their descriptions. At the 'Protein Pathways Game' level, students will play a game that involves identifying the sequence of organelles that are involved in the construction and transport of proteins.

Quote: The instructions, now held in RNA, must leave the nucleus and enter the cytosol to reach other organelles.

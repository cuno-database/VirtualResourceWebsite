States of Matter and Intermolecular Forces

Source: Khan Academy

Description: This progression of roughly 18 videos on states of matter and intermolecular forces is geared towards students preparing for the AP Chemistry exam. Topics include, but are not limited to, specific heat, vapor pressure, capillary action, solubility and intermolecular forces, and molarity of solutions.

Quote: "Let's say you had a container of liquid, and you wanted to increase the temperature, you'd probably add heat. But how much heat should you add?"

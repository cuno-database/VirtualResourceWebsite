Bond Breaker

Source: University of California, Irvine

Description: In this well-designed game by the Center at the Space-Time Limit (CaSTL) at UC Irvine, learn about subatomic particles, atoms, elements, bonding, intermolecular forces, heat, and lasers to navigate through microscopic mazes! You can even design your own levels to play. Comes with PDF lesson plans for teachers at both the middle school and high school level.

Quote: "An atom is ionized when an electron is removed, in this case knocked off by the energy of ultraviolet light."
